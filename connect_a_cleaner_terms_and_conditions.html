2.2.1. Businesses or contractors seeking to hire cleaners or
subcontractors can create proﬁles on Connect a Cleaner LLC to
search for and connect with individuals in the cleaning industry.

dashboard where they can search for cleaners within a speciﬁc
area. They can specify service requirements such as location, type
of service, and any additional information. Premium subscribers
have access to advanced features, including full information about
the cleaners and the ability to initiate connections for work
opportunities.
2.3. By providing a platform for individuals, businesses, and contractors,
Connect a Cleaner LLC facilitates eﬃcient and effective connections
within the cleaning industry, enhancing opportunities for work and
collaboration for all stakeholders.
