2.             Description of Services:

The objective of Connect a Cleaner LLC is to connect individuals, businesses, and contractors within the cleaning industry, as well as those interested in starting a career in cleaning, with opportunities for work and collaboration. Within the web application, there are three types of profile;les that can be created:

2.1.              Individual Profile:

People interested in the cleaning industry can create profile;les tailored to their preferences and skills. Whether you're a person seeking employment opportunities or an independent contractor/subcontractor looking for work, our web app caters to both.

2.1.1.               Individuals keen on pursuing opportunities within the cleaning industry or launching their careers in this ﬁeld can create its profile;les. These profile;les serve as a platform to highlight their skills, availability, and desired work locations. By showcasing their qualiﬁcations, individuals open themselves up to potential offers from businesses or contractors seeking to ﬁll job vacancies, contracts, or subcontracting opportunities.

2.1.2.               For independent contractors or subcontractors seeking employment within the cleaning industry, Connect a Cleaner LLC offers these profile;les to enable contractors to exhibit their availability, and expertise. By specifying their skills and preferred work areas, contractors can attract potential business partners or clients looking for additional workforce or subcontractors for various cleaning projects.

2.2.              Business Profile:

2.2.1.               Businesses or contractors seeking to hire cleaners or subcontractors can create profile;les on Connect a Cleaner LLC to search for and connect with individuals in the cleaning industry. Once a business creates an account, they gain access to a main dashboard where they can search for cleaners within a speciﬁc area. They can specify service requirements such as location, type of service, and any additional information. Premium subscribers have access to advanced features, including full information about

the cleaners and the ability to initiate connections for work opportunities.

2.3.              By providing a platform for individuals, businesses, and contractors, Connect a Cleaner LLC facilitates eﬃcient and effective connections within the cleaning industry, enhancing opportunities for work and collaboration for all stakeholders.
